The Bird and The Bear Bistro

August 4, 2013Houston, Texas

 

Mimosas and brunch on a sunday morning? What about The Bird and The Bear? This place is amazing. It has a wide variety of wines and a nice bar. If you are looking for quality, then you have to come to this place. Management is really nice to the customers, and if you come back they will remember you ;). I just have to say that I enjoyed the restaurant, and it is a great place to have a conversation with your friends and enjoy a good meal while feeling really classy
