Olympia Grill at Pier 21

August 1, 2013Galveston, Texas

 

Are you at the Pier 21 and you are new at Galveston? It's my first time visiting this city and I must say that this is a great spot to have lunch! The greek food is great and I must say that you won't regret having lunch at this place, also if you want to do some sightseeing there's a retired oil rig near this restaurant, and also there's a lot of boat tours around the coast. They also have a very cool soap in the restrooms.
